Getting Started

1

Download & Unzip

Click "Download .zip" above. Double-click the downloaded file to extract the DXF to XYZ.app.

2

Bypass Gatekeeper

Since this app isn't from the App Store, macOS will initially block it. Here's the fix:

① Right-click (or Control-click) the app icon

② Click "Open" from the menu

③ In the dialog, click "Open" again to confirm

You only need to do this once. After that, it opens normally.

3

Convert Your Files

Click Browse, select your DXF file, choose a laser preset, and hit Convert. Done!
