Help & Support

            
            

                
                
            


            

                

                    
1

                    

                        
Select your DXF

                        
Tap the drop zone to pick a file. On iPhone, this opens your "Files" app or Photo Library.

                    

                

                

                    
2

                    

                        
Choose a Preset

                        
Select your laser's spot size (UV is usually 0.06mm, Green is 0.07mm) to thin the point cloud.

                    

                

                

                    
3

                    

                        
Save Result

                        
Review the preview and stats, then tap "Save .xyz File" to store it on your device.
